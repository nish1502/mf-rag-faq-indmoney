# Sample Q&A for RAG-based Mutual Fund FAQ Chatbot

Below are some example questions and answers generated by the chatbot. All answers are based strictly on official documents from SBI Mutual Fund, SEBI, and AMFI.

---

### **1. Expense Ratio**
**Question:** What is the expense ratio for SBI Small Cap Fund?
**Answer:** All expense ratios are inclusive of GST on Management Fees. 
**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-small-cap-fund-329

### **2. Exit Load**
**Question:** What is the exit load for SBI Focused Fund?
**Answer:** The exit load for SBI Focused Fund is 12% for exit within 30 days from the date of allotment, and 0.25% for exit after 30 days and within 90 days from the date of allotment. 
**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-focused-fund-25

### **3. Minimum SIP Amount**
**Question:** What is the minimum SIP amount for SBI Large Cap Fund?
**Answer:** The minimum SIP amount applicable for SBI Large Cap Fund (formerly known as SBI Bluechip Fund) is Rs. 500. 
**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-large-cap-fund-(formerly-known-as-sbi-bluechip-fund)-43

### **4. Riskometer**
**Question:** What does the riskometer indicate for SBI Long Term Equity Fund?
**Answer:** The riskometer indicates that the risk of the scheme is Very High and the risk of the benchmark is Very High. 
**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-long-term-equity-fund-(previously-known-as-sbi-magnum-taxgain-scheme)-3

### **5. ELSS Lock-in Period**
**Question:** What is the lock-in period for ELSS?
**Answer:** ELSS schemes have a statutory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of allotment. 
**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-long-term-equity-fund-(previously-known-as-sbi-magnum-taxgain-scheme)-3

### **6. ELSS Tax Benefits**
**Question:** What are the tax benefits of ELSS?
**Answer:** ELSS schemes qualify for deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, allowing investors to claim a tax deduction of up to Rs. 1.5 lakh. These schemes also have a statutory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of allotment.
**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-long-term-equity-fund-(previously-known-as-sbi-magnum-taxgain-scheme)-3

### **7. Net Asset Value (NAV)**
**Question:** What is the NAV of SBI Small Cap Fund?
**Answer:** The NAV of SBI Small Cap Fund as on Mar 04, 2026 is ₹ 154.7423. It has a one day change of -1.84%. 
**Source:** https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-small-cap-fund-329
